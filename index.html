Rocktaves
is one of the most esteemed rock competitions in the country and endeavours to strengthen the fairly nascent Indian rock scene. Rocktaves is, in fact, responsible for launching some of the finest bands in the Indian rock scene today — Parikrama, Prestorika, Them Clones, The Superfuzz and Euphoria, to name a few. Get a chance to battle it out with the best and make your tryst with the legend.

    Rules
  1. Bands will get 25 minutes on stage (including sound check).
  2. There will be no language restrictions on the entries.
  3. No registration fees will be charged and accommodation will be provided.
  4. The selection of teams will be the sole discretion of BITS-Pilani.
  5. The final decision regarding any matter lies in the hands of the organizers and judges.
  6. Professionals will be handling the judging of the event.
  7. Any team violating any of the above rules is subject to direct elimination.
  8. Professional sounds shall handle the event. Drum kits will be provided.

    Axetacy is a showcase of guitaring prowess. Show off your inner Vai, Page, or McKee in an attempt to wow both the adoring crowd and the discerning judges. Axetacy is a solo guitar competition for both acoustic and electric guitarists. Registration will be done on the spot.
  1. Preliminary participants will not have more than 10 minutes each on stage, including tuning and sound check, etc. There is no restriction on the number of songs they can play.
  2. A basic sound setup will be provided. Participants are required to get their own guitars and patches/pedals/processors for the event. However if a guitar can be provided to a participant on the spot, it will be.
  3. Backing tracks are permitted in the preliminary stage, though not in the final round.
  4. The final round will be a face-off style event, where two contestants will be pitted against each other.
  5. A professional judge will be present.
  6. The decision of the judge and the organisers will be final and absolutely binding.
